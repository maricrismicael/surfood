How we help?

Through SurFood businesses can minimize the amount of food waste they produce by allowing people to avail their surplus goods at a much lower price.

In 2017, 2 million kilograms of food wastes were being produced daily in the capital city of the Philippines alone in which restaurants and food businesses are the second largest contributors next to households.

Stores of partnered companies of SurFood nationwide, will be available in the website in which consumers can purchase products that are 20% less than its original price. 75 percent of the total revenue goes to the partnered companies while the remaining percentage goes to SurFood.
